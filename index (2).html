About Renava Networks

Your Trusted Partner in Business Growth

Renava Networks Ltd is a fast-growing microfinance institution committed to financial inclusion in Kenya. Founded in 2022, we believe that every entrepreneur — no matter how small — deserves a genuine chance to succeed with dignity.

Headquarter in Nairobi Kenya, we serve markets, traders, women groups, boda boda operators, and small business owners with flexible, affordable loan products backed by a team that truly cares.

What We Offer

Flexible Financial Solutions

Tailored loan products for every business stage and need. From sole traders to group enterprises.

Business Loans

From KSh 30,000 to KSh 5 Million for your growing enterprise. Flexible repayment terms up to 24 months.

  • No collateral for small loans
  • Group and individual options
  • 24-hour approval process
  • Competitive interest rates

Asset Financing

Finance your equipment, boda boda, shop stock, or any income-generating asset with affordable monthly payments.

  • Boda boda & matatu financing
  • Equipment & machinery
  • Shop fixtures & stock
  • Low interest rates

Group Lending

Perfect for women groups, chamas, SACCOs, and community enterprises. Joint liability model with lower rates.

  • Women groups welcome
  • Minimum 5 members
  • Financial literacy training
  • Community-focused approach

Emergency Loans

Urgent cash needs? Access quick emergency loans disbursed within hours. For genuine business and personal emergencies.

Agricultural Loans

Seasonal crop financing, farm input loans, and agribusiness support aligned with your harvest cycle and repayment capacity.
